# 18 · Investor Pitch Summary

> The pitch in words — the narrative behind a deck. Numbers here are directional
> placeholders to be replaced with sourced, defensible figures before any raise.

## One-liner
**DLC OS is the open-source, AI-native operating system for commerce** — letting any
business sell and manage customers across web, Discord, WhatsApp, Telegram, and
social media from one dashboard they own.

## The problem
Commerce fragmented across channels, but the tooling didn't keep up. Businesses
duct-tape 8–15 disconnected SaaS tools — a store, brittle chat bots, separate
inboxes, a CRM that won't sync, email/SMS tools, dashboards nobody reads. Data is
siloed, customers repeat themselves, and owners have no single source of truth.
They pay the integration tax in fees and headcount.

## The solution
One open-source core with a unified data model, an AI assistant in the middle that
can *see and act on* the whole business, and native commerce on every channel
customers actually use. **Bigger than a store — an operating system for commerce.**

## Why now
- **Conversational/community commerce** is exploding; the channels (Discord/Telegram/
  WhatsApp) now expose commerce-capable APIs.
- **AI crossed the capability line** — it can genuinely sell, support, forecast, report.
- **SaaS fatigue + open-source trust** — businesses want to *own* their stack.
- **No one owns the intersection.** Incumbents are channel-blind; bots are shallow;
  AI tools are silos.

## Why we win (moat)
1. **Unified data model + AI** — one brain across every channel; hard to retrofit.
2. **Channel-native by design** — adding a channel is one adapter; incumbents can't
   match without cannibalizing their model.
3. **Open & ownable** — trust, adoption, and a contributor/plugin ecosystem that compounds.
4. **Wedge → platform** — we start where no one competes (Discord/AI) and expand
   across the whole stack each business already pays for.

## Go-to-market
- **Beachhead:** Discord/Telegram-native & creator sellers (technical, vocal, OSS-friendly).
- **Engine:** a lovable, demoable wedge + build-in-public + world-class DX → bottom-up adoption.
- **Expansion:** web, WhatsApp, CRM, marketing, marketplace — land and expand across channels and modules.
- Detail: [OSS Growth Strategy](./16-open-source-growth-strategy.md).

## Business model
Open-core. Free, fully-featured **AGPL-3.0** core forever; revenue from a **commercial license**, **managed Cloud** (primary),
**Enterprise** (SSO/audit/residency/SLA), **plugin marketplace** take-rate, and
**managed AI**. Proven model (GitLab, Supabase, Cal.com). Detail:
[Monetization](./17-monetization-strategy.md).

## Market
Large, growing intersection of e-commerce platforms + conversational commerce + AI
business tools. **TAM** in the hundreds of $B/yr; **SAM** tens of $B/yr; an
addressable **SOM** beachhead of $100M+/yr. Detail: [Market Analysis](./02-market-analysis.md).
